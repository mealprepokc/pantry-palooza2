Pantry Palooza – iOS App Store Submission Checklist
===============================================

Core App Identifiers
--------------------
- App Name: Pantry Palooza
- Bundle Identifier: com.pantry.palooza
- Bundle Prefix: com.pantry
- Apple ID / Team: (use your Apple Developer Program account)
- Support Email: support@pantrypalooza.com
- Privacy Policy URL: https://pantrypalooza-web.vercel.app/privacy-policy (publish docs/privacy-policy.md here)

Certificates & Provisioning
---------------------------
1. Create an App Store distribution certificate on developer.apple.com > Certificates.
2. Generate a production provisioning profile for bundle ID com.pantry.palooza.
3. Download the certificate (.cer) and provisioning profile (.mobileprovision) for future builds.

App Store Connect Metadata
--------------------------
- Subtitle (30 char max): e.g., "AI-powered pantry planner"
- Description: Outline pantry tracking, AI dish generation, dislike filters, shopping lists.
- Promotional text (optional): e.g., "Plan meals faster with AI-tailored recipes."
- Keywords: pantry, meal planning, recipes, AI cooking, grocery list
- Primary Category: Food & Drink (recommended)
- Secondary Category: Lifestyle (optional)
- Support URL: https://pantrypalooza-web.vercel.app/support
- Marketing URL: https://pantrypalooza-web.vercel.app/
- Age Rating Questionnaire: complete in App Store Connect.

App Privacy Responses
---------------------
Match answers to privacy policy:
- Data collected: Email, authentication tokens, pantry items, dislikes, analytics event data.
- Data use: App functionality, analytics, support.
- No tracking, no advertising networks, no sale of data.
- Children: Not directed to under 13.

Compliance Forms
----------------
- Export compliance: answers "No" (no custom encryption beyond HTTPS).
- Content rights: confirm you own / have rights to all content.
- Advertising identifier: select "No" (app does not use IDFA).

Visual Assets
-------------
- App icon: 1024 × 1024 PNG (transparent layers removed).
- Screenshots:
  • 6.7" display (iPhone 15 Pro Max) – 3–10 images.
  • 6.1" display (iPhone 15/14) – 3–10 images.
  • Optional 5.5" display (iPhone 8 Plus) for legacy layout.
- Optional App Preview video (up to 30 seconds, 1080 × 1920, H.264).

Build & Submission
------------------
1. Ensure versioning: update app.json with new version/build (e.g., 1.0.1 / build 2).
2. Generate IPA:
   npx eas build --platform ios --profile production
3. After build completes, submit using:
   npx eas submit --platform ios --profile production
   (or upload .ipa via Transporter).
4. Add TestFlight testers (internal/external) and gather feedback.

Review Notes
------------
- Provide demo credentials if login required (email/password).
- Mention AI-generated recipes are informational only; verify cooking temps.
- Highlight data deletion pathway: Account tab ➜ Delete my data (or email support@pantrypalooza.com).

Post-Submission
---------------
- Monitor App Store Connect > My Apps > Activity for review status.
- Address reviewer feedback promptly.
- Once approved, choose manual or automatic release.
